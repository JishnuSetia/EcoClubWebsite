I would like to extend a warm welcome to our esteemed guests from the Office of the Crown Prince, His Highness Sheikh Ahmed Bin Faisal Al-Qassimi. Your presence here today reflects a shared vision for a greener, healthier future, and we are grateful for your support in our endeavours.

HH Sheikh Ahmed Bin Faisal Al Qassimi is a member of the Ruling family. He anchors various public and private ventures in UAE spanning across sectors like water, energy, Agriculture, mining, banking, infrastructure, education and hospitality. He is a recipient of the Mother Teresa International Award for Culture and Administration.

They have embarked on a green sustainability drive of planting 1 million plants in UAE in the year 2024 which enhances the commitment that this country had made towards the global community during the COP28.

The importance of sustainability cannot be overstated. Our planet faces unprecedented challenges - climate change, pollution, and resource depletion - threatening our very existence, health, and future. Ignoring sustainability is akin to playing Russian roulette with our planet; temporary luck may prevail, but dire consequences loom. Thus, as individuals, communities, and nations, embracing sustainability becomes imperative.

At GEMS Modern Academy, we embody this commitment through various initiatives like the aeroponics garden, the planter's patch, the revive project, project carter, recycling collection drives, Biofuel project and many more.

Finally, this leads to the spread of awareness in the community and in the society.

Conclusion: Just as these trees will take root and grow, so will our commitment to environmental stewardship. Each tree represents a pledge to nurture and protect our planet, ensuring that it thrives for centuries to come.

In conclusion, let us embark on this journey of environmental responsibility with enthusiasm and determination. Together, we can create a legacy that transcends time, leaving a lasting impact on our school, our community, and the world at large.
